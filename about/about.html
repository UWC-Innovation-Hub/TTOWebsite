Innovation and Technology Transfer at UWC

The University of the Western Cape has a distinctive academic role in helping build an equitable and dynamic society. We are committed to excellence in research, nurturing the cultural diversity of South Africa, and responding in critical and creative ways to the needs of a society in transition. And at the UWC Technology Transfer Office we are passionate about creating additional value from the traditional academic process!

The UWC TTO serves as a link between academia, industry, and other innovation stakeholders.

As a team we are passionate about what we do and this is reflected in our core values: Integrity, collaboration, excellence, and passion.

Through our collective effort we can assist our researchers in:

  • ▪ Protecting and managing Research Outputs with the potential to create socio-economic value
  • ▪ Facilitating the transfer and commercialisation of these Research Outputs through collaborations, licensing, sales or spinouts
  • ▪ Sourcing of innovation and development funding
  • ▪ Supporting the promotion of entrepreneurship and innovation
  • ▪ Awareness on Intellectual Property
  • ▪ Compliance with relevant legislations
  • ▪ Facilitating research relationships and outcomes by means of research contracts

Together, we can go from hope to action through knowledge.

To learn more about the Technology Transfer Office, please contact a member of our team or email tto@uwc.ac.za.
