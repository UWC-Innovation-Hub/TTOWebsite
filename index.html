Protecting UWC’s Intellectual Property

Empowering innovation through responsible IP management

Overview

The Technology Transfer Office (TTO) supports researchers and innovators at UWC in protecting, managing, and commercializing intellectual property. This ensures that innovations developed within the university create real-world impact while preserving ownership and recognition.

Why Protect IP?

  • Safeguard novel ideas and research outputs.
  • Enable licensing and commercialization opportunities.
  • Attract industry partnerships and investors.
  • Ensure proper recognition and benefit-sharing for inventors.

How to Get Started

  1. Submit an Innovation Disclosure Form.
  2. TTO will evaluate the novelty and potential of your innovation.
  3. We’ll assist in filing patents or other IP protections if applicable.

For guidance, contact the TTO at tto@uwc.ac.za.
